## 📝 Study Plan & Advice

Here’s the recommended roadmap for this semester:

### 🤖 [Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML)](https://github.com/Hitme02/5th-SEM-CS-Cluster/tree/main/AIML%20(IS353IA))

* Focus primarily on the **notes**.
* The **Notion notes** are completely simplified and exam-ready.
* For numerical concepts, watch [**Mahesh Huddar**](https://www.youtube.com/@MaheshHuddar) on YouTube (links are embedded in the Notion notes).
* 🎧 **Pro-Tip:** Podcast-style revision works exceptionally well for this subject.

### 🗄️ [Database Management Systems (DBMS)](https://github.com/Hitme02/5th-SEM-CS-Cluster/tree/main/DBMS%20(CD252IA))

* [**Gate Smashers**](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLxCzCOWd7aiFAN6I8CuViBuCdJgiOkT2Y&si=XADnvLMP0UVaSLj5) on YouTube is an absolute lifesaver. They are the best resource out there, especially for **Normalization** and **Transactions** concepts.
* For study material, the **PPTs** provided are more than enough. You can also refer to the **Notion notes** as an alternative.

### 🧮 [Theory of Computation (TOC)](https://github.com/Hitme02/5th-SEM-CS-Cluster/tree/main/TOC%20(CS354TA))

* **Warning:** This is a tough, logical subject that usually comes really hard in exams. Dedicate more study time to it!
* Start with [**Sudhakar Atchala**](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLXj4XH7LcRfBkMlS_9aebcY78NLFwhE4M&si=LzrozQ7KGRwv6li3) on YouTube to build your foundations, but *don't rely entirely on his videos*.
* The **CIE-wise breakup notes** provided in the repo are a genuine lifesaver for exams—stick to them closely.

### ☁️ [Electives (Cloud Computing)](https://github.com/Hitme02/5th-SEM-CS-Cluster/tree/main/ELECTIVE%20(XX355TBX))

* Highly recommended as the best and easiest elective!
* Requires minimal effort: **2 hours before the CIE** (on the day of the exam) and **1 day before the SEE** is plenty.
* 🎧 **Pro-Tip:** Podcast-based revision can easily be done for this, even if you are studying it for the very first time.

### 📈 [Principles of Management Economics (POME)](https://github.com/Hitme02/5th-SEM-CS-Cluster/tree/main/POME%20(HS251TA))

* This subject is heavily theory and diagram-oriented.
* Focus purely on the **Notion notes** provided.
* Try to start studying **one week before the CIE exams**. 
* 🎧 **Pro-Tip:** Podcast-style revision is highly effective for memorizing theory here too.

## 🌟 Pro Tips for This Semester

* **Don't take TOC lightly!** It is the most challenging logical subject this semester—double down on it and rely on the CIE-wise breakup notes.
* **Podcast-style revision** is a massive cheat code this semester. Use it for AIML, Cloud Computing, and POME to reinforce your concepts on the go.
* **Diagrams matter!** Especially in POME.
